Name Change

Cloud and Crash were originally released by Hasbro as Torch and Midnight respectively. However, they besides their general shape, they are completely different Metal Wheels since Cloud and Clash both weigh more than their Hasbro counterpart. This is the reason the names are different: they are not really the same parts.
