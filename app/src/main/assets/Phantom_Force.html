Phantom Force is a series of Beyblades produced by Hasbro. They are recolours of previously released Beyblades with the differences from the previous releases being that there is a translucent tint in all plastic parts of the Beyblade, the plastic is stronger and has a lower friction coefficent.

Phantom Force Beyblades:

  • Bearing Stinger (Translucent Green-Blue)
  • Dragoon S (Translucent Dark Blue) (The tip is made of a harder rubber than any previous Dragoon S)
  • Driger S (Translucent Black)
  • Dranzer S (Translucent Red)
  • Wolborg (Translucent Cloudy Yellow)
  • Galeon (Translucent Purple)
  • Seaborg (Translucent Pink)
  • Draciel S (Translucent Yellow)
