Plastic Beyblade

From Beywiki, the Beyblade Encyclopedia - Presented by the World Beyblade Organization

Plastic Beyblade is a slang term invented by the Beyblade community used to refer to any Beyblade belonging to a system that predates the Heavy Metal System.
