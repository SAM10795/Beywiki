BBA Championship - Beywiki, the Beyblade Encyclopedia - Presented by the World Beyblade Organization BBA Championship From Beywiki, the Beyblade Encyclopedia - Presented by the World Beyblade Organization The BBA Championship line is a series of Beyblades produced by Hasbro. There were two runs of the series. The first are recolors of previously released Beyblades, while the second run was produced during G Revolution and consisted merely of re-releases of previously released Beyblades, such as Driger S and Dragoon V2, in some cases with slight part alterations.
