Random Booster Vol.1 Contents


Contents of MFB Random Booster Vol.1 Secret Aries

Note: There is no prize Beyblade in this booster. One whole beyblade is given in each box. There is 1/8 chance to get each beyblade.

  • Aries 125D (Secret Beyblade)
  • Pegasis 145F
  • Pegasis 145SF
  • Bull 105S
  • Bull 105D
  • Leone 125S
  • Sagittario 145SF
  • Sagittaro 145D


All Tracks and Bottoms found in this booster are the clear versions.

Availability

Random Booster Vol.1 Secret Aries was released in extremely limited quantities, with only approximately 1000 cartons being shipped (24 Boosters per carton), which resulted in only about 24,000 RBV1's being produced, and even fewer Aries 125D's.
