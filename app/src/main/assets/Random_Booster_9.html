Random Booster 9 Box

This Random Booster contains one random Attack Ring, one random Weight Disk and one random Blade Base with the possibility of forming the Beyblades listed below. The random chances offers a possibility of 300 color variations.


Contents of Random Booster 9

  • Galeon 2 (Prize Beyblade) (Black) 1/12
  • Trygator (Prize Beyblade) (Green) 1/12
  • Trygle 2 (Prize Beyblade) (Orange) 1/12
  • Galeon 2 (Jungle Green)
  • Galeon 2 (Sunshine Orange)
  • Trygator (Dark Black)
  • Trygator (Sunshine Orange)
  • Trygle 2 (Dark Black)
  • Trygle 2 (Green Jungle)
  • Flash Leopard (Army Green)
  • Metal Dranzer (Deep Aqua - Blue)

Weight Disks: Star Attack, Revolver Attack, Ten Heavy, Ten Balance or Ten Wide
