Hyperblades

From Beywiki, the Beyblade Encyclopedia - Presented by the World Beyblade Organization

Hyperblades is a series of Beyblades produced by Hasbro. They are recolors of previously released Beyblades with two different colors split through the center. The gimmick is designed so that when spinning, the two colors appear to mix together to produce a different color. The boxes they came in had a golden tint.

Hyperblades:

  • Driger F (Light Green/Silver-Green)
  • Dragoon F (Orange/Yellow)
  • Draciel F (Turquoise/Light Gray)
  • Galzzly (Periwinkle/Purple)
  • Galman (Green/Black)
  • Wolborg 2 (Purple/Rose Madder)
  • Seaborg 2 (Green-Yellow/Deep Jungle Green)
  • Master Dranzer (Red/Gold)
