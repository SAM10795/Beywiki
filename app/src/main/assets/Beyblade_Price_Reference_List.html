Beyblade Price Reference List

From Beywiki, the Beyblade Encyclopedia - Presented by the World Beyblade Organization

The Beyblade Price Reference List gathers costs for official Beyblade products that would be reasonable both for the seller and for the customer Blader, notably based on observed experiences. The numbers seen in the "Estimated Current Price" column may therefore be used to establish deals for trades or to contact a seller with a fair offer. These values take into consideration initial rarity, generational rarity, competitive usefulness, and original price at release. They however generally do not include shipping costs, although the latter usually vary around $10. The Estimated Current Prices are written in United States Dollars (USD), and it must be understood that the value of the yen shown in the "Initial Price at Release" column has most likely varied since, but in an effort for neutrality and factual data, only the raw prices given by TAKARA-TOMY or Hasbro were taken. Lastly, the following list provides prices for New In Box (NIB) items; used or mint but opened Beyblades should therefore cost less.
