Metal Master

From Beywiki, the Beyblade Encyclopedia - Presented by the World Beyblade Organization

Metal Master is a series of Beyblades produced by Hasbro. They are recolors of previously released Beyblades with a slight change in color tone. The ARs are coated with a metallic color while the WDs are plated (both based of the color of the Beyblade).

Metal Master:

  • Galzzly (Metallic Red AR/Red Plated Eight Wide)
  • Galman (Metallic Purple AR/Purple Plated Eight Heavy)
  • Master Driger (Copper AR/Copper Plated Eight Wide)
  • Metal Draciel (Metallic Black AR/Black Plated Eight Heavy)
  • Kid Dragoon (Copper AR/Copper Plated Eight Wide)
  • Dragoon F (Silver AR/Silver Plated Eight Wide)
  • Draciel F (Metallic Green AR/Green Plated Eight Balance)
  • Driger F (Silver AR/Silver Plated Eight Balance)
  • Dranzer F (Metallic Blue AR/Blue Plated Eight Heavy)
