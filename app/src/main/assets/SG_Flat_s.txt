SG Flat - Beywiki, the Beyblade Encyclopedia - Presented by the World Beyblade Organization SG Flat From Beywiki, the Beyblade Encyclopedia - Presented by the World Beyblade Organization Contents 1 Beyblades with this part 2 Overall 3 Related Articles Weight: 5 grams This is often considered the successor of the Flat BB. The flat tip allows for high movement speed, while the low height allows for effective Upper Attack. Beyblades with this part Guardian Driger Metal Dranzer Galzzly Master Dragoon Kid Dragoon Orca Diver Death Gargoyle / Dark Gargoyle Seaborg 2 Appollon Trygator Overall Although it is usable if there is a lack of better options, SG Flat is severely outdated and outclassed by parts such as the faster Storm Grip Base (Dragoon S)and the faster, longer-lasting SG Metal Flat 2 (Gaia Dragoon V).
