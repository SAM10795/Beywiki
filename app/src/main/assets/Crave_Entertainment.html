Crave Entertainment

From Beywiki, the Beyblade Encyclopedia - Presented by the World Beyblade Organization

Crave Entertainment, is an American video game publisher established in 1997. They specialize in budget, and imported game titles. They are notable for publishing the first Beyblade video game outside of Japan.
