Metal Master - Beywiki, the Beyblade Encyclopedia - Presented by the World Beyblade Organization Metal Master From Beywiki, the Beyblade Encyclopedia - Presented by the World Beyblade Organization Metal Master is a series of Beyblades produced by Hasbro. They are recolors of previously released Beyblades with a slight change in color tone. The ARs are coated with a metallic color while the WDs are plated (both based of the color of the Beyblade).
