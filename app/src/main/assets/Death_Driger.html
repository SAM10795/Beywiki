Hasbro released this Beyblade under the name Shadow Driger.

Attack Ring (AR): Star Shape

As the name suggests, Star Shape is in the shape of a star. It is one of the smallest ARs ever made. There is no competitive uses for Star Shape, except on WD Attack customizations, which are essentially useless in competitive play as well.

Use in WD Attack Customization

Star Shape's only possible use is in WD Attacking customs.

  • AR: Star Shape (Death Driger)
  • WD: Wide Defense
  • SG: Neo Right SG (MG Core)
  • BB: SG Metal Flat 2 (Gaia Dragoon V)

Weight Disk (WD): Eight Balance

See Eight Balance.

Spin Gear (SG): Right SG

See Right SG.

Blade Base (BB): SG Sharp Base

See SG Sharp.

Note: This SG Sharp is different than other SG Sharp models. It has clips that extend far enough to make the outside of the SG Sharp base a perfect circle.

Overall

With a lacklustre Attack Ring and no other parts of competitive note, there is little reason for any competitively-minded Blader to purchase Death Driger. This Beyblade is only worth buying for collection purposes.
