Survival - Beywiki, the Beyblade Encyclopedia - Presented by the World Beyblade Organization Survival From Beywiki, the Beyblade Encyclopedia - Presented by the World Beyblade Organization Survival (also known as Stamina and Endurance) is a classification of Beyblade that refers to Beyblades that battle by taking many hits and continuing to spin. These Beyblades usually have pointed tips and/or free-spinning Parts to lower friction. Zombie Beyblades are a popular sub-category of Survival Beyblades seen in Plastic and Heavy Metal System Beyblades.
