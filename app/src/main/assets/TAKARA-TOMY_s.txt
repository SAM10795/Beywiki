TAKARA-TOMY - Beywiki, the Beyblade Encyclopedia - Presented by the World Beyblade Organization TAKARA-TOMY From Beywiki, the Beyblade Encyclopedia - Presented by the World Beyblade Organization Takara is the Japanese company that created Beyblade. They are also known for Transformers and B-Daman, among other toy lines. In 2006, Takara merged with Tomy, becoming TAKARA-TOMY.
