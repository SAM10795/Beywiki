Light Wheel - Beywiki, the Beyblade Encyclopedia - Presented by the World Beyblade Organization Light Wheel From Beywiki, the Beyblade Encyclopedia - Presented by the World Beyblade Organization Light Wheels, which are a part of the Hybrid Wheel System, are included in Random Booster Light Volumes. They are deemed illegal by WBO rules, and are not meant for competitive use. Many Light Wheels are plastic versions of their Metal Wheel counterparts. Light Wheels are Metal Wheel substitutes made entirely of plastic. As a result, they are significantly lighter (weighing approximately 5 - 6 grams) and are essentially useless.
