BBA Championship

From Beywiki, the Beyblade Encyclopedia - Presented by the World Beyblade Organization

The BBA Championship line is a series of Beyblades produced by Hasbro. There were two runs of the series. The first are recolors of previously released Beyblades, while the second run was produced during G Revolution and consisted merely of re-releases of previously released Beyblades, such as Driger S and Dragoon V2, in some cases with slight part alterations.


BBA Championship:

  • Trygle (Turquoise)
  • Trypio (Green)
  • Master Dragoon (Red-Orange)
  • Black Dranzer (Recolor of Dranzer F)
  • Master Draciel (Cardinal)
  • Master Driger (Burgundy)
  • Wing Defenser (Light Cyan)
