Random Booster 4 Box

This Random Booster contains one random Attack Ring, one random Weight Disk and one random Blade Base with the possibility of forming the Beyblades listed below. This iteration focuses on SG Beyblades and the secondary recolors are meant to be two-toned colour versions. The random chances offers a possibility of 200 color variations.
