Attack - Beywiki, the Beyblade Encyclopedia - Presented by the World Beyblade Organization Attack From Beywiki, the Beyblade Encyclopedia - Presented by the World Beyblade Organization Attack is a Beyblade type used to classify aggressive Beyblades that battle by hitting the opponent repeatedly in order to stop them from spinning or to knock them out of the Beystadium. Attack-type Beyblades often have flat tips, sometimes made out of rubber in order to increase their speed. They generally attack using projections for Smash Attack or slopes for Upper Attack.
