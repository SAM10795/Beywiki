Random Booster 10 Box

This Random Booster contains one random Attack Ring, one random Weight Disk and one random Blade Base with the possibility of forming the Beyblades listed below. The random chances offers a possibility of 300 color variations.


Contents of Random Booster 10

  • Killer Eagle (Prize Beyblade) (Blue) 1/12
  • Rushing Boar (Prize Beyblade) (Light Green) 1/12
  • Death Gargoyle (Prize Beyblade) (Orange) 1/12
  • Killer Eagle (Field Green)
  • Killer Eagle (Bloody Red)
  • Rushing Boar (Blizzard Blue)
  • Rushing Boar (Bloody Red)
  • Death Gargoyle (Blizzard Blue)
  • Death Gargoyle (Field Green)
  • Seaborg 2 (Submarine Grey)
  • Trypio (Light Purple)

Weight Disks: Star Attack, Revolver Attack, Ten Heavy, Ten Balance, Ten Wide
