Full Fat

From Beywiki, the Beyblade Encyclopedia - Presented by the World Beyblade Organization

Full Fat, is an independant United Kingdom based video game developer. Established in 1996, they are known for their handheld games. Contracted through Atari, they developed Beyblade video games for the Nintendo GameBoy Advance durring 2003, and 2004.

Beyblade Video Games Developed by Full Fat

  • Beyblade: Ultimate Blader Jam
  • Beyblade: G-Revolution
